You Are Not Alone: The Prospect Is Imaginary

At the heart of all things, there is a mystery which makes them so.

You are this ineffable presence, yet you imagine yourself something else. The reason for this is simple: only through separation can there be things to do. This vast cosmos is art for its own sake.

The ego fears reunification because it sees in this the annihilation of itself. The ego is not right but it is also not wrong.
